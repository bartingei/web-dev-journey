Multimedia University of Kenya

MMU is a chartered university established with an act of parliamentin 2003

The university is built along magadi road on your way to Ongata Rongai town the university offers a variety of courses that are approved by the commission for university education

There are six faculties which include the following

Faculty of Business and Economics (FoBE)


Faculty of Computing and Information Technology (FoCIT)


Faculty of Engineering and Technology (FoET)


Faculty of Media and Communication (FAMECO)


Faculty of Science & Technology (FoST)


Faculty of Social Sciences and Technology (FoSST)
