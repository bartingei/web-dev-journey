About Us

KLADI is more than just a clothing brand — it’s a mindset. Born out of passion for self-expression, KLADI blends street style with attitude to create fashion that doesn’t follow the crowd — it leads.

Every collection is crafted with bold ideas, clean cuts, and just the right amount of edge. Whether you’re on the move or in your zone, our pieces are designed to fit you — your mood, your grind, your vibe.

We stand for confidence, creativity, and the kind of authenticity you don’t have to explain. KLADI is built for those who wear their identity proudly and dress like they mean it.

Welcome to the future of everyday fashion. Welcome to KLADI.

Our Story

Kladi was born from a passion for sustainable fashion and the thrill of discovering unique vintage pieces. We believe that great style doesn't have to come at the expense of our planet.

Our team scours thrift stores, estate sales, and vintage markets to bring you carefully curated clothing with character and history. Each piece is inspected, cleaned, and photographed with care.

By choosing secondhand, you're not just getting unique fashion - you're reducing textile waste and supporting a more sustainable future for fashion.

Our Mission

To empower self-expression through bold, sustainable fashion that speaks without words.


Our Vision:

To become a leading force in urban fashion, known for creativity, sustainability, and authenticity worldwide.

The Team Behind KLADI

We’re a small crew of designers, dreamers, and fashion rebels who believe in doing things differently and better.
