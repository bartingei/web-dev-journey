Our Story

Founded in 2010, Safari Flavors brings the authentic taste of Kenya to the heart of Nairobi. Our journey began with a simple mission: to celebrate Kenya's diverse culinary heritage through carefully crafted dishes made with locally sourced ingredients.

Today, we're proud to be one of Nairobi's most beloved dining destinations, offering a warm atmosphere where guests can enjoy traditional flavors with a contemporary twist.
